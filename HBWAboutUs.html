About Us


Welcome to our company profile, use this page to familiarise yourself with our staff members and to learn more about how our company works.


We are a web development team which focuses on using various types of programming languages for developing websites. We mainly focus on making use of programming languages including: JavaScript, Python, HTML (HyperText Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), all of which are most commonly used in web development. We have many jobs available, such jobs include: UI (User Interface) Design, Website Testing, Web Design, Coding etc. Be sure to look at our Courses page before applying. For enquiries, contact us through email, facebook or by giving us a call, contact details are available on the home page of this website.


Here at Hot Beans Web, we believe that team work is the best way of making sure that our website are created as quickly as possible, and to make it easier for our colleagues to think of the best possible designs. Team work will also allow employees to get to know each other better, we want to make sure that our colleagues feel comfortable in our working environment. We ensure that all of our websites are functional through the help of our website testers in order to guarantee customer satisfaction. We are currently on the road to gain popularity for companies intending to establish their official website. However, due to the increasing amount of workload due to the increase in popularity, we are now in the lookout for new website developers, so if you are reading this and are looking to start your career as a website developer, please consider joining us, we would we more than happy to introduce you to our workforce.


Our Team


Harris Thompson

Front-End Website Developer

Greetings user, my name is Harris, I am one of the company's front-end developers. As a front-end developer, my main responsibility is to construct the visual elements of a website, such elements which your are seeing right now on your screen, I am in charge of adding content on websites such as imagery, text and even interactive content such as scroll animations and interactive slideshows. In my opinion, I believe that being a front-end developer is a relatively fun job to have as it involves using your own creativity and imagination, it is sort of like creating digital art which is informative with the inclusion of text. Think of it as drawing a website design from the top of your head on paper then applying it on the computer screen, although drawing the website on paper would be much easier than creating one using coding oftware such as visual studio code, you have to make sure you know what each specific tag does as well as understand different coding languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. If you have experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I think you should have little to no problem with creating websites using these languages.
