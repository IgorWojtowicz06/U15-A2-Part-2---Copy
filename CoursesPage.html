Courses

On this page, we are offering you a list of courses to choose from. If you are interested have a read through our list of courses to make up your mind on what you would like to do once you join our company.



Front-End Website Development

Hourly Rate: £24.36

If you interested in constructing the visual display of a website and have an interest in art, this would be the right course for you. As a front-end developer, you will be focusing on using languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript to create the visuals of our websites, as well as add contents such as imagery, videos, text and interactive content on those websites. You will also be in charge of creating designs for those websites through wireframing, which involves creating a visual design to showcase what the websites will look like, you will also be speaking to clients to show them the wireframes which you have created in order to receive feedback from our clients.

Course Requirements:

Good knowledge of coding languages, especially HTML, CSS and JavaScript, GCSEs Maths and English at Grade C or above (Grade 4 or above), BTEC Level 3 qualification in Computer Science.

Back-End Website Development

Hourly Rate: £25.64

As a Back-End Website Developer, you will focus on the server-side of web applications, along with database management and cybersecurity. your job will primarily involve ensuring that all of the websites we make are safe for user interaction and input of information such as payment details when performing online transactions as well as the input of personal details while creating an account through a website, this is to ensure confidentiality of user information. Back-End website developers also have the responsiblity to build mechanisms which allow websites to process data such as user information. This will also involve coding, just like front-end website development.

Course Requirements:

Good understanding of programming languages such as C and C++, API-related knowledge, BTEC Level 3 qualification in Computer Science, GCSE Maths and English at grade C or above (Grade 4 or above)

Website Tester

Hourly Rate: £17.17

Website testers working at our company will be focusing on quality assurance for all of our websites, by testing the functionality of websites based on their performance, usability and clarity. Website testers will also provide feedback to the front-end and back-end website developers on what went well while using the websites and what went wrong and needs improvement. Website testers will be given test logs to fill out in order to determine which specific section of web page has worked sucessfully and which specific sections of the web page have failed and need to be addressed for further improvement. The websites will be tested more than once, until all of the standard user needs and client needs have been met, this is to make sure that our websites are 100% functional to provide our clients with top quality websites.

Course Requirements:

Sufficient understanding of testing tools such as Selenium and JUnit, BTEC Level 3 qualification in Computer Science, GCSE Maths and English at Grade C or above (Grade 4 or above)
